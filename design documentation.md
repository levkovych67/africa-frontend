Global Architectural Rules (The "Non-Negotiables")Before writing a single line of CSS, the frontend team must adhere to these absolute constraints:Zero Shadows: The box-shadow property is strictly forbidden across the entire application. Depth is established through contrast (black vs. white) and 1px borders.Brutal Geometry: border-radius: 0px globally. No exceptions for buttons, inputs, images, or drawers.Sub-Pixel Prohibition: All borders and layout calculations must resolve to integer pixels to avoid blurring on 4K/retina displays. Use floor() or round() in dynamic calculations if necessary.Cyrillic-First Rendering: Ensure crisp rendering of Ukrainian characters by applying text-rendering: optimizeLegibility; and -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased; to the global body tag.2.
